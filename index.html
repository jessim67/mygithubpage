About Me

My name is Jessi Carder and I am a junior at Virginia Tech with a major in Psychology and a minor in Computer Science. I am interested in how physical disabilities affect mental conditions and vice versa. I plan to attend graduate school so that I can one day work with the psychology community to improve testing procedures for autism spectrum disorders. In my free time I like to work with web design using HTML and CSS, and I hope to get involved with Android app design in the near future.
